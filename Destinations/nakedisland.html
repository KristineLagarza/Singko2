NAKED ISLAND

Surfing isn't only the only exciting activity you can do in the Surfing Capital. Siargao is also popular for island hopping in the Philippines. It comes as no surprise since Siargao has several pristine islets that are every beach lover's dream.

Naked Island tours feature one of the three islands you can see from the shores of General Luna. The other two islands are Daku Island and Guyam Island, which along with Naked Island, make up the highly-recommended island hopping tour in Siargao.
