Guyam Island

Joining an island-hopping escapade in Siargao should be on top of your list. As mentioned earlier, there are several idyllic islets off the coast of Siargao that you should experience. Aside from Naked Island, there's Guyam Island.

Picture a tiny tropical island in the middle of the Pacific Ocean as a grove of luscious palm trees circled by beautiful white sand and a lovely array of rock formations. That's Guyam Island.
